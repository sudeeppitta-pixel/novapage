Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a consultation cost? +
Your initial consultation is 100% free with no obligation. We review your case and advise you on the best path forward at no cost.
How long do I have to file a personal injury claim? +
In Texas, the statute of limitations for personal injury claims is 2 years from the date of the accident. Act promptly to preserve your rights.
What if I can't afford an attorney? +
We work on a contingency fee basis for personal injury cases — you pay nothing unless we win your case. No upfront costs, ever.
How long does a divorce take in Texas? +
In Texas there is a mandatory 60-day waiting period. Uncontested divorces can finalize in 2–3 months; contested divorces may take longer.
